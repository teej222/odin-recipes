Tacos

Main Page

Tacos are a staple mexican dish, ranging from authentic, to fast food versions across the globe. Today we will be preparing a version very similar to that of a fast food restaurant, but much higher quality.

Ingredients:

  • Taco shells
  • Ground beef or meat of choice
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Taco seasoning pack

Steps

  1. Cook beef for 8-10 minutes stirring occasionally until brown. Drain
  2. Stir salsa into beef, slowly lower heat to medium-low. Cook for 5 minutes
  3. Heat taco shells as directed on package. Serve taco shells with beef mixture, lettuce, tomato and cheese.
